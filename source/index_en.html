Temporarily block/unblock your credit cards
What if you misplaced your card or forgot where you put it. Simple. Log on to the HSBC Vietnam App and place a temporary block on your card and unblock it if your card turns up later. If it doesn't show up, you can report your card as lost and get a new replacement card by contacting us.
(Select your credit card > 'Manage cards'> 'Block or unblock credit card')
